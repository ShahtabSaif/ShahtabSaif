## 🙋‍♂️ About Me

Hi! I'm **Shahtab Hossain** — a developer and designer who loves bridging the gap between hardware and beautiful visuals. I work on everything from circuit boards to Canva designs and Android apps.

- 🎨 Designing graphics with **Photoshop**, **Illustrator** & **Canva**
- 🤖 Building with **Arduino** & **Raspberry Pi**
- 📱 Developing Android apps with **Kotlin**
- 💻 Coding with **VS Code** as my trusty editor

- 😄 Open to collaborations, freelance work & cool projects!
